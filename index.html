Chain Atlas

Blockchain Interoperability Directory

Chain Atlas is a growing tool focused on providing comprehensive information about blockchain interoperability and cross-chain technologies.

Our goal is to create an extensive database of blockchains, assembled through extensive research of blockchain bridges and their capabilities. The initial list of blockchains was compiled based on the 320 bridges' supported chains and major CEXes, including Binance, Binance.US, Coinbase, ChangeNOW, SideShift, and Gate.io. Additional sources such as Defillama, Chainlist.org, and Long List of Crypto were also utilized.

The research was conducted in November-December, and we continue to add any newly discovered chains and bridges. In the future, we plan to expand our database by including essential information like website, L1/L2 classification, ecosystem (EVM, Cosmos, Polka, etc), native token, and status (active, deprecated). If you'd like to participate or contribute to our project, please contact us on Telegram: @chainatlas.
